About Me

I received my Ph.D. degree from Computer Science Department,  University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign (UIUC) in December 2012. I got my M.S. degree in Signal and Information Processing in July 2007, and B.S. degrees in Computer Science and in Statistics in June 2004 from Peking University, China.
